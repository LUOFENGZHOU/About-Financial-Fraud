<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">李占国申辩认为：1.在审议包含1001项议案的董事会决议时，确实存在未尽审慎义务的问题，但董事会表决程序过于仓促，对其行使表决权造成了实质性影响。2.董事会秘书陆俊安向其告知，上交所不可能批准该次董事会决议的披露申请。因此，李占国认为标题混乱等问题会被交易所督促纠正，所以才同意董事会决议。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">我会认为，1.表决程序的仓促并非董事的免责事由，上市公司董事应按照法律要求，勤勉尽责地审议董事会决议，保证上市公司信息披露的真实、准确、完整。李占国所述未经充分审议即同意董事会决议的情形，充分印证了其本人疏于履职尽责的事实。2.李占国在明知董事会决议存在重大问题，无法得到上交所认可的情况下，仍然审核同意董事会决议，严重违背了董事的法定义务，突破了董事的职业底线。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">根据当事人违法行为的事实、性质、情节与社会危害程度，依据《证券法》第</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">一百九十三</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">条</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">第</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">一款、第三款</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">的规定，我会决定：</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">一、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">对</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">慧球科技责令改正，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">给予警告，并处以</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">60</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">万元罚款</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">二、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">对</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">鲜言</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">给予警告，并处以</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">90</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">万元罚款</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">其中作为直接负责的主管人员罚款30万元，作为实际控制人</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">罚款</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">60</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">万元</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">三、对</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">董文亮、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">温利华</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">、陆俊安</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">给予警告，并分别处以</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">30万元罚款。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">四、对</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">刘光如、李占国</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">给予警告，并分别处以20万元罚款。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">当事人应自收到本处罚决定书之日起15日内，将罚款汇交中国证券监督管理委员会（开户银行：中信银行总行营业部，账号：7111010189800000162，由该行直接上缴国库），并将注有当事人名称的付款凭证复印件送中国证券监督管理委员会稽查局备案。当事人如果对本处罚决定不服，可在收到本处罚决定书之日起60日内向中国证券监督管理委员会申请行政复议，也可在收到本处罚决定书之日起</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">6</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">个月内直接向有管辖权的人民法院提起行政诉讼。复议和诉讼期间，上述决定不停止执行。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
