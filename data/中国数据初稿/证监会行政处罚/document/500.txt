<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt" class=p0><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">以上违法事实</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">有</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">莱茵置业</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">公告</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">莱茵置业</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">的相关说明、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">相关证券</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">交易记录</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">吕顺龙的</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">谈话笔录等证据证明</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">足以认定</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>

<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt" class=p0><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">对</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">吕顺龙</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">处以3万元罚款。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
