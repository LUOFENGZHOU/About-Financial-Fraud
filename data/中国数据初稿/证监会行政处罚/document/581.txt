<P class=Custom_UnionStyle align=center><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>〔<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2012</SPAN>〕<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">6</SPAN>号</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT face=宋体><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">当</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">事人：</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">山东京博控股发展有限公司（以下简称京博控股），住所：山东省博兴县，法定代表人马韵升。</SPAN></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>马韵升，男，<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">1962</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">12</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2</SPAN>日出生，时任京博控股法定代表人、董事长，<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">住址：</SPAN>山东省博兴县。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>依据《中华人民共和国证券法》（以下简称《证券法》）的有关规定，我会对京博控股违反证券法律法规行为进行了立案调查、审理，并依法向当事人告知了作出行政处罚的事实、理由、依据及当事人依法享有的权利。当事人未<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">提出陈述、申辩意见，也未要求听证</SPAN>。本案现已调查、审理终结。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>经查明，京博控股存在以下违法事实：</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>一、京博控股未就交易国通管业股票按规定履行报告和信息披露义务并继续交易国通管业股票</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT face=宋体><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">7</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">24</SPAN>日，京博控股持有</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">安徽国通高新管业股份有限公司（以下简称国通管业）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">股票<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">4,203,914</SPAN>股，持股量占国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">6%</SPAN>，京博控股未向国务院证券监督管理机构和证券交易所就持有国通管业股票情况作出书面报告，未通知国通管业并予公告。</SPAN></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT face=宋体><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">7</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">24</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票超过国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">5%</SPAN>后，在未向国务院证券监督管理机构和证券交易所就持有国通管业股票情况作出书面报告，未通知国通管业并予公告的情况下，继续交易国通管业股票。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">8</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">3</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">10.37%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">8</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">10</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">15.12%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">8</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">27</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">20.24%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">9</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">24</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">25.10%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">12</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">13</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">30.09%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">12</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">18</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">30.31%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">4</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">15</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">25.23%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">12</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">18</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">20.13%</SPAN>。<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2009</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">4</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">9</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票占其已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">16.12%</SPAN>。京博控股在<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">7</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">25</SPAN>日至<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2009</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">4</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">9</SPAN>日期间未对持有国通管业股票情况予以真实披露。</SPAN></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>京博控股的上述行为违反了《证券法》第八十六条<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">和</SPAN>《上市公司收购管理办法》第十三条的规定。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT face=宋体><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">对上述行为</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">直接负责的主管人员为京博控股时任法定代表人、董事长马韵升。</SPAN></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>二、京博控股于<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">7</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">11</SPAN>日和<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">9</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">25</SPAN>日两次虚假披露关于持有国通管业股票的情况</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>京博控股<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">7</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">11</SPAN>日披露，其<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">7</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">10</SPAN>日持有国通管业股票<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">5,251,343</SPAN>股，持股量占国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">5%</SPAN>。经查，京博控股<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">7</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">10</SPAN>日持有国通管业股票<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">25,120,262</SPAN>股，持股量占国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">23.92%</SPAN>。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>京博控股<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">9</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">25</SPAN>日披露，其<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">9</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">24</SPAN>日持有国通管业股票<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">10,536,361</SPAN>股，持股量占国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">10.03%</SPAN>。经查，京博控股<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2008</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">9</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">24</SPAN>日持有国通管业股票<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">27,812,654</SPAN>股，持股量占国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">26.48%</SPAN>。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>京博控股的上述行为构成了《证券法》第一百九十三条所述违法行为。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT face=宋体><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">对上述行为</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">直接负责的主管人员为京博控股时任法定代表人、董事长马韵升。</SPAN></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>三、京博控股未向国通管业股东发出收购股份要约</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>京博控股<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">12</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">13</SPAN>日持有国通管业股票<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">21,069,256</SPAN>股，持股比例超过国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">30%</SPAN>，达到<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">30.09%</SPAN>。此后，京博控股继续收购国通管业股票，但未向国通管业所有股东发出收购股份的要约。直至<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">2007</SPAN>年<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">12</SPAN>月<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">21</SPAN>日，京博控股持有国通管业股票<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">20,505,108</SPAN>股，持股量占国通管业已发行股份的<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">29.29%</SPAN>，低于<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">30%</SPAN>的比例。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>京博控股的上述行为违反了《证券法》第八十八条的规定。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT face=宋体><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">对上述行为</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">直接负责的主管人员为京博控股时任法定代表人、董事长马韵升。</SPAN></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT face=宋体><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">以上违法事实，有相关证券账户开户资料、交易记录，资金划转凭证，相关机构的工商登记资料，临时公告，相关人员谈话笔录等证据证明</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt">，足以认定。</SPAN></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=Custom_UnionStyle><SPAN><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt" face=宋体>根据当事人违法行为的事实、性质、情节与社会危害程度，依据《证券法》第一百九十三条、第二百一十三条的规定，我会决定：</FONT></SPAN></P>
