<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">以上事实，有当事人询问笔录、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">当事人提供的相关资料、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">相关单位提供的资料、相关单位提供的情况说明</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">、工商登记资料、相关证人证言等证据证明，足以认定。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">我会认为，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">庄敏及</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">陈海昌、庄明、蒋俊杰</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">的上述行为构成了《证券法》</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">第</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">二百一十四条所述违法行为</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">中达股份的虚假信息披露行为违反</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">了《证券法》第</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">六十</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">三条</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">、第六十八条的规定。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; COLOR: rgb(0,0,0); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">根据当事人违法行为的事实、性质、情节与社会危害程度，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">我会决定</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">：</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
