<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">听证</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">中，当事人创势翔、黄平、张毅</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">提出</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">的主要申辩意见如下：</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">第一，当事人不具有操纵证券市场的违法故意。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">一是交易主管张毅擅自做了尾市拉抬及尾市集合竞价拉抬股价的违规行为，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">并有公司内部对张毅该行为的处罚材料予以证明；</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">二是2015年7月21日“汉缆股份”的交易行为，是因公司投资总监黄平与交易主管张毅出现交易意见分歧而产生，不具有主观故意。第二，本案违法所得的计算标准应以创势翔实施涉案交易行为实际获得的收益为准。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p17 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)">经复核，我会认为：</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)">第一，当事人明知或应当知道相关的交易行为足以导致相关股价的波动，而故意放任该行为的发生，其操纵意图明显。对于创势翔所提，交易主管张毅擅自所做尾市拉抬和尾市集合竞价拉抬，以及“汉缆股份”交易系因交易意见分歧而导致的申辩意见，没有客观证据支撑。对当事人该项申辩意见不予采纳。第二，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">《证券法》第二百零三条</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">规定的“没收违法所得”的范围，应当包括违法行为所产生的全部收益，至于这种收益是否属于违法行为主体创势翔，不影响本案操纵行为违法所得的认定。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)">创势翔对产品的收益是否具有处分权，不影响我会对创势翔违法行为的认定和处罚。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">根据当事人违法行为的事实、性质、情节与社会危害程度，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">依据</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">《证券法》</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">第二百零三条的规定，我会决定：</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">一、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">没收创势翔违法所得20,</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">73</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">8</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">,393</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">.</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">32</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">元，并处以62,215,179.96元罚款</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">二、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">对黄平给予警告，并处以</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">60</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">万元的罚款</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=p0 style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">三、</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">对张毅给予警告，并处以</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">10</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">万元的罚款。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal; FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
